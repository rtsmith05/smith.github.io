ABOUT US

HideAWay Dog Grooming started in 2010 in a small two car garage at Randall Smith's house in Raleigh

 With no local advertising involved, the small shop grew in popularity well above any of the local competing

stores. Through simple word of mouth and business cards, the business exploded overwhelming the staff

and the owner. Reservations and appointments are made two to three months in advance. Custom clientel

was a staple, meaning appointments had to be met or consequences were strong. Offering not just pet grooms,

but Show, Custom, and Asian Fusion grooms as well. Competitive pricing and a friendly, professional staff 

will guarantee you and your pet will  have the best grooming experience in Raleigh!!
